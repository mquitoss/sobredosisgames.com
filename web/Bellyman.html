Bellyman

The world is in danger, the invading hordes of evil dimension are trying to conquer the world. The United Nations has requested help to the super hero league, but they are in the other side of the galaxy. So they had to call Bellyman, God help us.

But with your help, Bellyman can save the world. Bellyman has a secret weapon ... its extraordinary metabolism turns fast food (burguers, donuts, beer) in powerful shock waves. You can launch them as projectiles, destroying everything.
